About OBARAIBI

We are a money lending company that provides single and double-digit business loans to green economy businesses and offers tailored solutions for individuals' health, education, travel, and housing needs.

Our Story

In 2016, we identified an opportunity in the financial system for individuals and businesses to quickly drive change and achieve more, thereby positively impacting the continent. Our agile and innovative team saw this change as an opportunity to impact the world by aiding individuals, and in doing so, we recognized the importance of ensuring a sustainable Green African economy.

Beyond Money Lending

Our agile money lending systems ensure seamless and discreet lending innovation, tailored to the specific needs of each individual customer.

Privacy Policy

Our end-to-end privacy policy guarantees that borrowers' identities and financial dealings with us remain confidential, with no third-party involvement.

Our Little Secret

We believe that every individual with some financial resources has the ability to positively impact their immediate world. We find it rewarding to encourage and support this ability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide financial freedom through personalized loan services that respect our clients' privacy and adapt to their lifestyles. We aim to simplify the borrowing process, making it user-friendly, and accessible to all.

We believe in empowering our clients by offering multiple loan options, flexible repayment plans, and a commitment to discretion. At OBARAIBI, your financial journey is our priority.
