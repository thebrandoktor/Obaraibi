Why Choose Us

1. Privacy: Don't worry; we don't discuss other people's businesses. Because you prefer to keep your business discreet, we have taken this even further with our End-to-End Policy; all your business transactions remain discreet with us.

2. Multiple Loans: You can always get another loan, even with an ongoing one. Because we understand, we have evolved a Simultaneous Loan Policy to enable you access multiple loans simultaneously. This means you can apply for another loan even when you have one or two unpaid.

3. Easy Payment: You can pay however and whenever you want, in parts or as a whole, daily, weekly, or however you choose, within the loan tenure.

4. Peace of Mind: Relax! We won't call or text you; just pay whenever you can within the period you choose.

FAQs

What are the eligibility requirements? +
Our basic requirements include being at least 18 years old, having a regular source of income, and a valid bank account. Plus you need to have a valid government issued Identification(NIN,or Passport) and have a Collateral
What can I Use as Collateral? +
You can use Cars,Laptops, Household Appliances, Luxury Goods etc. Landed Properties are yet to be accepted; and a chat with our loan officer will provide more clarity on this for you.
How long does the approval process take? +
As Soon as Collateral is inspected and validated
What is the minimum amount I can borrow ? +
Our loan amount starts from N50,000.
Can I make mutiple loan applications? +
Yes, you can make as many applications as you want.
What are the interest rates? +
Our interest rates vary based on loan type, term length, your credit profile, and market conditions. Current rates range start from 5.99% to 27.99% depending on the tenure you want.
